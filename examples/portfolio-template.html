About Me

A multidisciplinary creative who thrives at the intersection of design and technology.

My Story

With over eight years of experience crafting digital products, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies. My approach combines user-centered design thinking with deep technical expertise.

I started my career as a front-end developer, then fell in love with design. Today, I bridge both worlds -- turning abstract ideas into pixel-perfect, performant experiences that users love.

When I'm not designing or coding, you'll find me exploring Barcelona's architecture, experimenting with generative art, or mentoring junior designers at local meetups.

My Process

A proven four-step workflow that turns ideas into polished, impactful products.

01

Discover

We start with deep research -- understanding your users, business goals, competitors, and constraints to define a clear strategy.

02

Design

Wireframes evolve into high-fidelity prototypes. Every screen is tested, iterated, and refined until it feels just right.

03

Develop

Clean, performant code brings the design to life. I build with accessibility, responsiveness, and scalability at the core.

04

Deliver

Launch day is just the beginning. I provide documentation, training, and ongoing support to ensure long-term success.
