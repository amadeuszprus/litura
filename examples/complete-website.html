FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlowMetrics. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

How long does it take to set up FlowMetrics?

Most teams are up and running in under 15 minutes. Our SDK supports all major frameworks, and our visual event editor means you can start tracking user actions without writing custom code. Enterprise customers with complex setups typically complete onboarding in under a week with help from our dedicated team.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes. Our Starter plan is completely free and includes up to 10,000 events per month, 3 team members, and core analytics dashboards. It is perfect for side projects, MVPs, and early-stage startups exploring product-market fit.

How does FlowMetrics handle data privacy and compliance?

We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully support GDPR and CCPA requirements. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You can configure data residency to keep information in specific geographic regions. We also offer on-premise deployment for Enterprise customers with strict data sovereignty requirements.

Can I migrate from another analytics platform?

Absolutely. We provide migration guides and tools for all major platforms including Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, and Heap. Our support team can also assist with custom migrations for Enterprise plans. Historical data import is available on Growth and Enterprise tiers.

What integrations are supported?

FlowMetrics integrates with over 200 tools including Segment, Stripe, Salesforce, Slack, HubSpot, Intercom, Jira, and many more. We also offer a robust REST API and webhooks for custom integrations. New integrations are added every month based on customer feedback.

What kind of support do you offer?

Starter plans include community support through our forums and documentation. Growth plans include priority email support with a 4-hour response time. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager, Slack-based support, and guaranteed 1-hour response times for critical issues.
